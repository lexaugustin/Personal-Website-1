About Me

Hello!

I am Alexander Augustin, an undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at the University of Florida and a tech enthusiast. Latest Projects

Gaming Trivia

Gaming Trivia is an iOS trivia app about video games. The user gets to answer 10 questions, and he or she gets 1 points for each correct answer, but currently, the app doesn't have enough questions to make it fun enough for public use. I am also working on a better version focusing more on education, which will include categories like geography, science and software development questions. The app is not available in the App Store, but the source code is available on my GitHub account.
